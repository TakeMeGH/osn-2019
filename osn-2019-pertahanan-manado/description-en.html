Description

The city of Manado is being attacked by a disease outbreak. Pak Dengklek, as the Manado physician, concludes that Manado residents need a healing potion which can be formulated from N types of medicine, numbered 1 to N.

The biggest medicine shop in Manado sells all types of medicine. Each type costs S rupiahs per bottle. Besides, the shop sells M packets of medicine, numbered 1 to M. The i-th packet contains the A[i]-th medicine type to the B[i]-th medicine type, one bottle each. The i-th packet costs P[i] rupiahs.

Help Pak Dengklek to determine, what is the cheapest price to buy at least one bottle for each of the N medicine types, to save Manado residents from the disease outbreak.

Input Format

The input is given with the following format: 

N M S
A[1] B[1] P[1]
A[2] B[2] P[2]
.
.
.
A[M] B[M] P[M]

Output Format

A single line containing the cheapest price to buy at least one bottle for each of the N medicine types.

Explanation of Sample 1

In this sample, Pak Dengklek can buy the first (costing 2 rupiahs) and the second (costing 1 rupiah) packets of medicine, and buy the third medicine type separately (costing 3 rupiahs). Therefore, Pak Dengklek spends 6 rupiahs and collects one bottle for each of the N medicine types. There is no way for Pak Dengklek to spend less than 6 rupiahs.

Explanation of Sample 2

In this sample, Pak Dengklek can buy all packets of medicine and spends 5 rupiahs.

Subtasks

For all subtasks:

  • 1 ≤ N ≤ 200,000.
  • 1 ≤ M ≤ 200,000.
  • 1 ≤ S ≤ 1,000,000.
  • 1 ≤ A[i] ≤ B[i] ≤ N.
  • 1 ≤ P[i] ≤ 1,000,000.
  • N ≤ 200.
  • M ≤ 16.
  • N ≤ 200.
  • M ≤ 200.
  • N ≤ 2,000.
  • M ≤ 2,000.
  • P[i] = S.
  • No additional constraints.
